Privacy Policy

Custom Rings  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information Custom Rings collects, how it's used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Custom Rings is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not operate servers, create user accounts, or collect personal information.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Ring names, targets, and daily progress values you create
  • Cluster names and organization
  • Daily history snapshots
  • Streak counts
  • App preferences and settings

Apple Health Data

If you choose to use Auto-Track rings, Custom Rings requests read-only access to specific Apple Health data types you select — such as step count, active calories, exercise minutes, or sleep. This data is:

  • Read directly from the Health app on your device
  • Used only to display your progress within the app
  • Never transmitted off your device
  • Never shared with third parties

You can revoke Health access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Custom Rings.

In-App Purchases

Custom Rings offers a one-time Pro upgrade through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics & Advertising

Custom Rings does not include any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties — because we never collect it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Custom Rings does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no user accounts, no data collection, and no advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
